# NDA Access Path

The private Consequence Tank engine may be shared only after written approval. For commercial review, investor diligence, technical pilot work, platform evaluation, logistics evaluation, or model-provider review, an NDA may be required before access.

## Normal Flow

1. Review this public proof kit.
2. Send an access request using [ACCESS_REQUEST.md](ACCESS_REQUEST.md).
3. Confirm the legal person or organisation requesting access.
4. Sign an NDA if requested.
5. Receive limited access to the agreed private materials.
6. Keep all private materials confidential.
7. Agree separate commercial terms before production, resale, embedding, hosting, or external deployment.

## Private Materials

Private materials may include:

- private repository source
- private release artifacts
- simulation internals
- analytics and evidence implementation details
- governance ledger and verifier internals
- unpublished roadmap
- unpublished notebooks
- private evidence bundles
- commercial pilot materials

## Confidentiality Boundary

Do not copy, publish, train on, scrape, redistribute, sublicense, upload, or expose private materials to another system, person, model provider, company, hosted service, or commercial product unless the written agreement permits it.

## Access Can Be Revoked

Access may be revoked if the request was inaccurate, the use changes, confidentiality is breached, or commercial use begins without written permission.
